### 2. [resemble-ai/chatterbox](https://github.com/resemble-ai/chatterbox)

### 3. [jellyfin/jellyfin-desktop](https://github.com/jellyfin/jellyfin-desktop)

### 6. [simstudioai/sim](https://github.com/simstudioai/sim)

### 7. [C4illin/ConvertX](https://github.com/C4illin/ConvertX)
